The Story
A Jungle Mystery
with a Twist Ending

Panic in the jungle. Every animal has seen the monster, but no two descriptions match.

 

The brave donkey goes to find the truth.

 

It was their own reflections all along.

Written for PlayShifu's free eBook library, this 19-page story lives inside the Oko jungle universe from the Plugo Letters STEM kit. Each animal's clue - "it's pink!", "furry mane!", "it echoes!" - is secretly their own reflection distorted by the Banyan Tree's roots. A gentle, funny lesson in perspective and courage for kids 4–8.
